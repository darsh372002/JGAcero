RECTOR VESSLES

JG Acero is a leading manufacturer specializing in the fabrication of high-quality reactor vessels designed for various industrial applications, including chemical processing, petrochemical, pharmaceutical, and power generation industries. Reactor vessels are critical components used to carry out controlled chemical reactions under specific conditions of temperature and pressure.

Reactor Vessels at JG Acero are engineered with precision, using advanced materials and fabrication techniques to ensure durability, safety, and optimal performance. The company emphasizes strict adherence to international standards such as ASME Section VIII for pressure vessels, ensuring compliance with rigorous safety and quality requirements.

  • Reactor vessels manufactured at JG Acero can range from a few tons to over 100 tons depending on the size, material, and design requirements. Typical weights for medium to large vessels can be between 5 tons to 75 tons or more.
  • Diameter: Usually varies from 1 meter (3.3 ft) to 6 meters (20 ft) or larger for very large industrial reactors.
  • Height/Length: Can range from 2 meters (6.5 ft) to over 15 meters (50 ft) depending on design specifications.
