HORIZONTAL TANKS

At JG Acero, we design and fabricate high-performance horizontal storage tanks engineered for safe containment and efficient handling of liquids and gases across various industrial applications. These tanks are widely used in sectors such as Oil & Gas, Chemical, Pharmaceutical, Food Processing, and Water Treatment. Built using premium-grade stainless steel or carbon steel, our tanks are corrosion-resistant, leak-proof, and built to last under high-pressure or high-temperature conditions.

  • Material Options: SS304, SS316, MS, IS2062-grade carbon steel
  • Wall Thickness: 4 mm to 20 mm (depending on application pressure and volume)
  • Orientation: Horizontal cylindrical shape with optional dished/elliptical ends
  • Usage: Storage of fuels, chemicals, solvents, water, edible oils, and more
Weight Varies from ~500 kg to 8+ tons (as per size & spec)
Dimensions Length up to 12 meters; Diameter varies from 0.6 to 3 meters (based on capacity)
Capacity Range From 500 liters to 100,000 liters (customizable)

PETROLEUM TANKS

At JG Acero Industries Pvt. Ltd., we specialize in the fabrication of high-performance petroleum tanks designed for safe storage and transport of diesel, petrol, and aviation fuels. Our tanks are custom-built to meet industry standards for structural integrity, corrosion resistance, and pressure handling.

Manufactured using high-grade carbon steel or stainless steel (as per client requirements), each tank undergoes rigorous quality checks including hydro-testing, radiography, and surface treatment to ensure leak-proof performance and extended service life.

  • Material: Carbon Steel / Stainless Steel (304 / 316 as per application)
  • Wall Thickness: 6mm to 12mm (depending on capacity and pressure requirements)
  • Internal Finish: Pickled and passivated (for stainless), or epoxy lined (for carbon)
  • Fittings: Manhole, drain valve, fuel gauge, breathing valve, lifting hooks, level indicator
Weight Ranges from 1.5 tons to 6 tons based on size and fittings
Testing Hydrotest at 1.5x pressure, ultrasonic and visual weld inspection
Capacity Range 1,000 to 50,000 liters (customized on request)

PLANETARY MIXURE BOWL

At JG Acero Industries Pvt. Ltd., we manufacture high-performance Planetary Mixer Bowls designed for efficient mixing, blending, and processing of semi-solid and viscous materials across industrial applications. Our mixer bowls are engineered for durability, hygiene, and ease of handling — making them ideal for use in pharmaceutical, food processing, chemical, and cosmetic industries.

Constructed from high-grade stainless steel (SS304 or SS316), each bowl is precision-fabricated to match the planetary mixer head it’s paired with, ensuring secure fitting, smooth rotation, and seamless operation. Designed with a mirror-polished internal surface for easy cleaning and resistance to contamination, our mixer bowls meet strict quality and GMP compliance standards.

  • Material: SS304 / SS316 Stainless Steel
  • Wall Thickness: 2 mm to 4 mm (as per application)
  • Finish: Internal – Mirror Polish; External – Matte/Satin
  • Bottom valve (butterfly or ball type),Handle and trolley base for mobility
Weight 12 kg to 50 kg depending on size and material
