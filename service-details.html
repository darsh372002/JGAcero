Service Overview

At JG Acero Industries Pvt. Ltd., we offer comprehensive in-house fabrication services including designing, sheet rolling, plasma cutting, machining, assembly, and surface treatment. Our services are tailored to manufacture precision process equipment like pressure vessels, heat exchangers, tanks, and reactors. With ISO 9001:2008 certification and modern facilities in Nashik and Pune, we deliver quality-engineered solutions for critical industries such as Oil & Gas, Pharmaceuticals, Chemicals, and Food Processing. Every project we handle reflects our focus on quality, customization, and long-term performance.

At JG Acero Industries Pvt. Ltd., we offer end-to-end in-house services tailored to precision fabrication. Our expertise begins with advanced designing using CAD tools to ensure process-ready engineering layouts. We perform high-accuracy sheet rolling and CNC plasma cutting to shape metals for vessels and assemblies. Our machining services deliver tight tolerances and premium surface finishes for critical components. We handle complete fabrication and assembly of process equipment with skilled craftsmanship, followed by specialized surface treatments like buffing, pickling, and passivation to ensure corrosion resistance and compliance with industry standards. Together, these services form the backbone of our commitment to quality, durability, and performance across industries.
