WHO WE ARE?

Established in 2013, JG Acero Industries Pvt. Ltd. is an ISO 9001:2008 certified company specializing in precision-engineered stainless steel and carbon steel process equipment. With over 100 successful projects, we serve industries such as Oil & Gas, Pharma, Biotech, and Chemical. Backed by a skilled team and facilities in Nashik and Pune, we are trusted by government bodies, MNCs, and leading industrial clients across India.

WHAT WE DO!
  • Stainless steel and carbon steel pressure vessels
  • Storage tanks, fermentation tanks, reactors, and heat exchangers
  • Complex column assemblies, machining components, and structural fabrications
  • Spares and after-sales support for custom-built equipment

COMPANY HISTORY

A TEAM OF RELIABLE AND EXPERIENCED CONTRACTORS

Foundation & Vision

Established with a vision to deliver high-quality, reliable fabrication for process industries, starting with a modest team and workshop in Nashik.

2013

First Major Projects

Successfully executed projects for leading pharmaceutical and chemical clients; built early trust through precision and commitment.

2014-16

Scaling Operations

Expanded to a second facility in Pune and increased team strength. Took on larger projects (up to 500 tons) and entered government and petroleum sectors.

2017-19

Quality & Certifications

Achieved ISO 9001:2008 certification, strengthened internal QA processes, and began preparing for U-STAMP, R-STAMP, and PESO certifications.

2020-22

Trust & Retention

Achieved a rare milestone — zero customer loss in 10 years. Built a solid base of repeat clients, and refined our identity as a “trusted transformation partner.”
