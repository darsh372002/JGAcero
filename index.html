WHO WE ARE?

Established in 2013, JG Acero Industries Pvt. Ltd. is an ISO 9001:2008 certified company specializing in precision-engineered stainless steel and carbon steel process equipment. With over 100 successful projects, we serve industries such as Oil & Gas, Pharma, Biotech, and Chemical. Backed by a skilled team and facilities in Nashik and Pune, we are trusted by government bodies, MNCs, and leading industrial clients across India.

WHAT WE DO!
  • Stainless steel and carbon steel pressure vessels
  • Storage tanks, fermentation tanks, reactors, and heat exchangers
  • Complex column assemblies, machining components, and structural fabrications
  • Spares and after-sales support for custom-built equipment

Plasma Cutting

Machining

Fabrication & Assembly
